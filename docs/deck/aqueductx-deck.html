Between the third of the world's food smallholders grow and the markets that consume it stands a chain of intermediaries, because trade runs on judgment—an operational cost a single smallholder lot cannot carry.

07Data model

The data model is REA—the Claim is the point.

Sell and finance are one schema, not two features. The model captures the smallholder economy whole—what the farm sells, and what it consumes to produce.

AqueductX's types are a Resource–Event–Agent model. Naming it turns "map the value chain" from a slogan into schema decisions, and surfaces the one object that was missing: the Claim, financing's deferred half.
