## Thesis

Nature finance is investable but bottlenecked on credible, cheap biodiversity KPIs. The EU B&B reports say it explicitly: "biodiversity metrics remain nascent," and the conclusion calls for "satellite monitoring, drones, IoT, and AI to scale and lower the cost of generating nature KPIs." The **marine** version of that gap is acute — state-of-nature data is expensive, fragmented, low-resolution, and corporate-marine tools are scarce. Regen Atlas already owns the defensible layer (typed provenance, RAEIS attestation NFTs, bioregional aggregation, scientific valuation, Scout/Diligence agents). The 2026 commodity wave — Earth foundation models (Earth Index, Google Farmscapes), in-browser GIS (GeoLibre), proof-of-location (Octet/Astral), eDNA ground-truth (SimplexDNA) — supplies everything else. **We assemble; we don't rebuild.** "MRV is the missing rail" is the narrative.
